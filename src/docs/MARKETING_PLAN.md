# 📈 Complete Marketing Plan - Sobriety App

## 🎯 Executive Summary

**Goal**: Acquire 10,000 active users in 90 days with focus on high-intent audiences seeking recovery support.

**Budget Recommendation**: 
- Starter: $1,500-3,000/month
- Growth: $5,000-10,000/month
- Scale: $15,000-30,000/month

---

## 📊 Target Audience Profiles

### Primary Audience
- **Age**: 25-55 years old
- **Demographics**: All genders, all income levels
- **Behavior**: Actively searching for recovery tools, privacy-conscious
- **Pain Points**: Lack of accountability, isolation, tracking difficulty

### Secondary Audiences
1. **Family/Friends** - Looking to support loved ones
2. **Healthcare Providers** - Therapists, counselors, AA sponsors
3. **Corporate Wellness** - HR departments, EAP programs

## 📊 KPIs & Tracking

### Primary Metrics

| Metric | Target | Tracking |
|--------|--------|----------|
| CAC (Cost per Acquisition) | < $3.00 | Ad platforms |
| Install to Onboard Rate | > 60% | Analytics |
| Day 7 Retention | > 40% | Firebase |
| Day 30 Retention | > 25% | Firebase |
| Free to Premium Conversion | > 5% | Stripe |

### Attribution Setup

1. **UTM Parameters** for all ads:
   - utm_source: google/facebook/instagram
   - utm_medium: cpc/cpm/organic
   - utm_campaign: [campaign_name]
   - utm_content: [ad_variation]

2. **Conversion Events** to track:
   - app_install
   - onboarding_complete
   - first_checkin
   - day_7_active
   - premium_subscribe

## 📞 Need Help?

This marketing plan is designed to be executed in phases. Start with Google Search (highest intent) and Facebook awareness, then expand as you gather data and optimize.

Key success factors:
1. **Test early, test often** - A/B test everything
2. **Focus on retention** - Acquisition means nothing without retention
3. **Tell real stories** - Authentic testimonials convert best
4. **Respect privacy** - This audience values discretion
5. **Measure what matters** - Don't get distracted by vanity metrics
