2. **Quick Actions** - Emergency button, mood check, meditation access
3. **Achievement Badges** - Milestone celebrations with earned badges
4. **Weekly Report** - Summary card with stats and progress

5. **Mood Check-In** - Emoji selection with mood slider
6. **Sleep Tracker** - Sleep quality input with trends

8. **Daily Goals** - Checklist of wellness activities

### Triggers & Coping (4 screens)
9. **Trigger Logger** - Form for logging triggers with intensity
10. **Pattern Analysis** - Charts showing trigger patterns
11. **Craving Timer** - Countdown timer with breathing exercise

### Progress & Stats (4 screens)


## 📝 Brand Voice

- **Tone**: Supportive, encouraging, non-judgmental
- **Language**: Simple, warm, empowering
- **Messaging**: Focus on progress, not perfection
- **Avoid**: Clinical terms, shame-based language, triggers
